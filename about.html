Fab Steel Engineering Services is a structural steel detailing consultancy that provides accurate, fabrication-ready drawings and models for a wide range of steel structures. We support steel fabricators, EPC contractors, and general contractors by using Tekla Structures to deliver detailed 3D models, connection drawings, material take-offs, and erection plans that meet international standards.

We provide detailing solutions for the industrial, commercial, offshore, and infrastructure sectors. This includes pipe racks, equipment support structures, platforms, stairs, handrails, gratings, ladders, and offshore skids.
