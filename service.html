Services
OUR SERVICES

Structural Steel Solutions: Tailored for Precision & Performance

Structural Steel Detailing

Preparation of accurate fabrication drawings, shop drawings, and GA drawings using Tekla Structures for structural and miscellaneous steel.

Connection Detailing

Expert detailing of bolted and welded connections, base plates, gusset plates, stiffeners, and moment connections based on client design standards.

Miscellaneous Steel

Detailed drawings for stairs, handrails, ladders, gratings, and platforms, with shop-fit and installation-friendly documentation.

Offshore Structure Detailing

Specialized detailing services for offshore containers, skids, frames, and support structures compliant with DNV 273 / ADNOC / API standards.

Material Take-Off (MTO)

Detailed material take-offs and bill of materials (BOM) for procurement and estimation, exported from Tekla.

Erection Drawing and Marking Plans

GA and erection drawings with clear bolt plans, part markings, and member tags for efficient on-site assembly.

Rebar & Anchor Bolt Detailing

Anchor bolt setting plans and embedded steel items for cast-in-situ concrete foundations.

Model Coordination & Clash Detection

Clash checks and 3D coordination with other disciplines (if IFC/DWG/STEP files provided), ensuring constructibility and model integrity.
