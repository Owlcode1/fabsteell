PROCESS
Work Process

OUR 4-STEP WORK PROCESS FOR STRUCTURAL STEEL PROJECTS

1

Project Review & Client Coordination

We begin by reviewing the project requirements, structural drawings, and specifications. Our team coordinates with clients and design engineers to clarify the scope and technical inputs

2

Steel Detailing & 3D Modeling

Using Tekla Structures and industry standards, we create precise 3D models, connection details, and shop drawings. Clash checks and model reviews ensure accuracy before approval.

3

Drawing Submission & Revisions

We deliver fabrication drawings, GA drawings, MTOs, and erection plans for client review. Based on feedback, we revise and finalize all documentation for fabrication.

4

Support & Project Handover

We provide continuous support during fabrication and erection. Final documentation, bolt lists, and IFC files are handed over for a smooth project closeout.

Industries We Serve

STRUCTURAL STEEL SOLUTIONS FOR DIVERSE INDUSTRIES

Fab Steel Engineering Services is a trusted partner delivering accurate steel detailing, 3D modeling, and fabrication documentation for various sectors. Our Tekla-based solutions enhance construction workflows, improve build accuracy, and reduce rework — across industries that demand precision and reliability.

OIL & GAS STRUCTURES

We deliver offshore and onshore platform steel detailing including skid structures, pipe supports, and modular units in compliance with DNV and client-specific standards.

Our detailing for refineries, storage facilities, and pipelines ensures durability and safety, meeting industry regulations and standards.

INDUSTRIAL BUILDINGS

From factories to processing plants, we provide detailing for heavy steel structures, equipment platforms, walkways, and ladders.

Our work on industrial facilities involves precise detailing for large-scale operations, such as manufacturing plants, warehouses, and distribution centers.

COMMERCIAL PROJECTS

Structural framing, staircases, mezzanines, and canopies for malls, offices, and mixed-use buildings with precise coordination.

From modern office towers to expansive retail spaces, our team ensures seamless integration of steel structures into commercial developments.

INFRASTRUCTURE

Bridges, substations, transmission towers, and utility structures — we support engineering consultants with accurate models and shop drawings.

From bridges and tunnels to airports and transportation hubs, we deliver steel detailing solutions that support complex infrastructure projects. Our expertise in this area ensures safe, reliable, and resilient structures that contribute to the long-term functionality of public infrastructure.

PEB - BUILDING

Custom detailing for PEB manufacturers including rafters, columns, purlins, girts, and bracing systems for warehouses and sheds.
